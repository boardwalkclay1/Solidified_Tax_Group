Privacy Policy

Solidified Tax Group is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding the personal and financial information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect, store, and use your information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to prepare your tax documents and provide related services. This may include identification documents, income forms, tax records, and contact information.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used solely for tax preparation, document processing, communication, and secure record‑keeping. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing or unrelated services.

Security Measures

We use secure storage, encrypted communication where applicable, hashed identifiers, and restricted access controls to protect your data. Uploaded documents are stored privately and are accessible only to authorized staff.

Your Rights

You may request access to your stored documents, ask for corrections, or request deletion of your information where legally permitted.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through the form on our Contact page.
